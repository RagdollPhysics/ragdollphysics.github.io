Current Projects
ZoneBuilder

This one is somewhat functional. (yay?)

ZoneBuilder is aimed at being a fully functional fastfile creator for IW4. It currently supports xanims, xmodels, materials, and rawfiles with clipMap support on the way as well as weapons and sounds as soon as I get around to it. With the new aIW3 I am currently working on this one and will be for the foreseeable future.

Website: https://github.com/TheApadayo/zonebuilder

T4M

A small modification to the T4 client in order to aid modders who were hitting asset limits, memory limits, etc. IT also adds some features like a full FOV slider and borderless windowed mode.

Website: https://github.com/DidUknowiPwn/T4M
